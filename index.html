[Trispace]

H i s t o r y

Trispace is a variable font created by Etcetera type co, The founder of which was the maker of League Mono. Wanting to stray from the original design of League Mono, the ETC team decided to make some more drastic changes to their original typeface. A goal in the creation of Trispace was to create the feeling of a monospace font while remaining slightly more readable. The final draft of Trispace was released to the public initially in August of 2018 with multiple static styles as well as one variable font. It is free for all to use and can be downloaded via Google Fonts.
